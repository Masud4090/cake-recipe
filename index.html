  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour your cake pan.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Whisk sugar, eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients gently.
  5. Pour batter into pan. Tap to remove bubbles.
  6. Bake for 30–35 mins or until toothpick comes clean.
  7. Cool in pan 10 mins, then wire rack.
  • Make it chocolate: replace ½ cup flour with cocoa.
  • Add-ins: chips, nuts, fruit zest.
  • Frost with buttercream, whipped cream, or ganache.
  • Store airtight in fridge up to 3 days.
